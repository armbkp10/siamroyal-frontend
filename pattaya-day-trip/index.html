Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How long is the drive from Bangkok to Pattaya?
Approximately 1.5 to 2.5 hours depending on traffic. We recommend departing by 7am to avoid Bangkok rush hour and make the most of your day.
Is Coral Island (Koh Larn) worth visiting?
Absolutely — Koh Larn has some of the clearest water near Bangkok. The short 30-minute speedboat from Pattaya pier brings you to beautiful beaches rarely crowded in the morning.
Can families with young children join?
Yes, Pattaya is very family-friendly. Nong Nooch Garden has shows and activities that children love. We can adjust the itinerary for your family's needs.
